- 규칙. S+V+O (주어+동사+목적어) 형태로서 중학교 3학년 수준의 문장일 것.  

### 1. 소유・상태 (~을 가지고 있다)
1. I have a new smartphone.  
2. She has a cute dog named Coco.  
3. We have many books in the classroom.
4. My brother has a blue bicycle.  
5. They have a beautiful garden behind their house.  
6. He has a lot of homework tonight.  
7. The school has a large swimming pool.  
8. I have some free time this afternoon.  
9. She has a great passion for music.  
10. We have a math test tomorrow morning.  

### 2. 식사・음료 (~을 먹다/마시다)
11. I usually have bread for breakfast.  
12. He had a glass of milk before sleeping.  
13. We had delicious pizza for dinner yesterday.  
14. She wants to have some ice cream.  
15. They always have lunch together at school.  
16. My family had special dinner at a restaurant.  
17. Do you want to have warm tea?  
18. I had an apple during the break.  

### 3. 경험・시간・일과 (~을 보내다/겪다)
19. We had a good time at the amusement park.  
20. I had a strange dream last night.  
21. She had a bad cold last week.  
22. They had a long discussion about the project.  
23. He had a small accident on his way home.  
24. We had a wonderful winter vacation.  
25. I had a hard day yesterday.  
26. She always has a busy schedule on Mondays.  

### 4. 특징・성질 (특징을 지니다/증상을 겪다)
27. He has brown eyes and dark hair.  
28. This room has two big windows.  
29. I have a terrible headache right now.  
30. She has a very kind heart.
